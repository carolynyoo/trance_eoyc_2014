Trance Top 20 Songs of 2014

2014 was the year I discovered and fell in love with trance. It was the year I saw the beauty in listening to 10+ minute extended mixes and saw the subtleties of synths & sidechaining. I fell in love with so many artists/DJs, jumped and sang along at many shows, & found the wonder that is psy/tech trance (but still couldn't help the guilty pleasure of vocals). As an homage to my first year of trance, these are my most played and/or most loved songs of 2014.
